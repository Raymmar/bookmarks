Welcome to BookmarkDB.

This is a powerful bookmark manager that integrates with X.com to turn your doom scrolling into deep insights. 

It allows you to connect to X.com to import bookmarks, or add them directly in the app, or with a browser extension. 

All bookmarks get summarized (Sent to Open AI for analysis including images) and auto-tagged so you can search and explore them visually. 

The graph page will show you an interconnected graph
